Event Horizon

    Controls
  • W to move up
  • A to move left
  • S to move down
  • D to move right
  • Q / E to switch weapons
  • P to pause
  • R to restart
  • Left click to shoot
About:

The goal of the game is to get as high of a score as possible. You'll earn points as you damage and defeat enemies. As your score increases, there will be difficulty modifiers added to the enemies that will make them more difficult to defeat.
