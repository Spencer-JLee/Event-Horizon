Event Horizon

    About:
  • Damage and defeat enemies to earn points
  • Score as many points as possible
  • The higher your score, the more difficulty modifiers will be added
  • Pick health and ammo to last longer
    Controls:
  • Press P to pause/unpause
  • Press R to restart when it's game over
  • Press W to move up
  • Press A to move left
  • Press S to move down
  • Press D to move right
  • Press Q / E to switch weapons
  • Left click for your player to shoot in that direction
